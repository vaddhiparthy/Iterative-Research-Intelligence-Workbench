# Code of Conduct

## Our Standard

Contributors should keep collaboration direct, respectful, and technically grounded.

Expected behavior:

- discuss problems concretely
- challenge ideas without attacking people
- keep pull requests and issues focused on the software
- avoid sharing secrets or private data in public threads

Unacceptable behavior:

- harassment or personal attacks
- doxxing or disclosure of private information
- spam or bad-faith issue filing

## Enforcement

The repository owner may remove content, close discussions, or block contributors who violate these expectations.
